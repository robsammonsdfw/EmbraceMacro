
# EmbraceHealth AI - Platform Documentation

## I. System Inventory (Current Active Modules)
1. **MacroVision Core**: Vision-based nutrient extraction (Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fat, Micros).
2. **MasterChef AI (Restaurant Mode)**: Deconstructs cooked meals into culinary recipes.
3. **PantryChef AI (Pantry Mode)**: Identifies raw ingredients and suggests 3 diverse recipes.
4. **ChefGPT: Cook Mode**: Full-screen, high-readability step-by-step guidance.
5. **Medical AI Planner**: Condition-specific (Diabetes, Hypertension, etc.) meal plan consolidation.
6. **Prism 3D Body Hub**: Readiness scores, HRV sync, and AI-powered exercise form analysis.
7. **Goal Architect**: Biometric-based target setting with automatic unit conversion (Metric/Imperial).
8. **Pantry Hub**: Shopping list management with Instacart & AmazonFresh integration.
9. **Professional Suite**: Coach Proxy system for remote patient/client management.
10. **Health Wallet**: Points-based rewards system for healthy habits.
11. **Social Sync**: Metadata-aware community feed and friend management.
12. **Barcode Engine**: Real-time product lookup via barcode scanning.

## II. User Instructions

### 1. Daily Tracking
- **Snap a Meal**: Tap the Camera icon. Select "Snap" for macros or "Dine" for a restaurant recipe reconstruction.
- **Manage Fridge**: Use "Pantry" mode to photograph your ingredients; the AI will tell you what to cook.
- **Scan Labels**: Use the "Scan" mode for store-bought items.

### 2. Physical Intelligence
- **Body Hub**: Check your "Ready Score" to see if you should push hard or rest.
- **Form Check**: Use "AI Form Check" during squats to get real-time posture corrections.
- **3D Scan**: Tap the Avatar to open the Prism 3D scanner for clinical-grade measurements.

### 3. Planning & Shopping
- **Medical Plans**: Go to "Meal Planner" -> "Generate AI Plan". Select your conditions (e.g., COPD + Heart Health).
- **Auto-Grocery**: In "Groceries", tap "Import" to pull all ingredients from your medical plan into your shopping list.
- **Delivery**: Tap the Instacart or Amazon logo to send your list directly to their checkout.

### 4. Coaching & Rewards
- **For Coaches**: Act as a Proxy for clients to build their plans remotely.
- **Earn Points**: Log meals (+50), track sleep (+20), and complete Daily Pulses (+15) to grow your Health Wallet.
